Bulgaria

Tourism in Bulgaria accounts for a significant share of the country's GDP. Most tourists visit the resort either summer,  either winter in accordance with the season. The prospect of tourism development in Bulgaria is bright, the country has both cultural and natural attractions. In 2015, the Daily Mail newspaper called the resorts in Bulgaria the cheapest in Europe. The grocery basket in the key Bulgarian resorts is much cheaper than the same in the resorts of Italy, Spain and Turkey. In addition, in Bulgaria there are only two non-tourist months - October and November. The Black Sea March is already a sure spring with blooming trees, in June, they bathe in the Black Sea with might and main - until September. The ski season begins in December and lasts until February.

Sozopol

Sozopol is located 34 km east-southeast of Bourgas. The city is located on a small peninsula, crashing into the Black Sea. Near Sozopol is the largest Bulgarian island in the Black Sea, Saint Ivan. Sozopol is a unique city of its kind, which combines a modern seaside resort with a leading cultural, historical and spiritual center for centuries to this day. Romantic with its old part, picturesque with its magnificent views, unique with its location!

Hotel"Morska Vila"

Family hotel "Morska vila " is located in a quiet and calm place, near to the exit of Sozopol - direction "Duni royal resort". The hotel is 100 meters away from the beach and offers a nice sea view from it's rooms and apartments. At guests disposal there are swimming pool, lobby bar, huge veranda with view at the sea and the town of Sozopol .Price includes parking place and swimming pool. Hotel "Morska vila" is consisting of 3 double rooms, 5 studios and 4 apartments. Luxury furnished all equipped with cable TV, air conditioner, fridge, bathroom and veranda with sea view.
